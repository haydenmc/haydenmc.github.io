This recipe is a family favorite from Paul Prudhomme. Cajun style spicy tomato
pasta sauce with chicken. Delicious!

# Ingredients
- Pasta (I like to use Linguine)
- Salt
- **Sauce Seasoning Blend**
    - 2 tsp - Dried Thyme Leaves
    - 1 1/4 tsp - Ground Cayenne Pepper
    - 1 tsp - White Pepper
    - 3/4 tsp - Black Pepper
    - 1/2 tsp - Dried Sweet Basil Leaves
- **Sauce**
    - 1 cup (2 sticks) - Unsalted Butter
    - 1 - White Onion
    - 6 - Minced Garlic Cloves
    - 2 - 32oz Boxes of Chicken Stock
    - 2 tbsp - Worcestershire Sauce
    - 1 tbsp + 1 tsp - Tabasco Sauce
    - 2 - 16oz Cans of Tomato Sauce
    - 2 tbsp - Sugar
    - 2 cups - Green Onions, Chopped
- **Chicken Seasoning Blend**
    - 1 1/2 tbsp - Salt
    - 1 1/2 tsp - White Pepper
    - 1 1/2 tsp - Garlic Powder
    - 1 1/4 tsp - Ground Cayenne Pepper
    - 1 tsp - Black Pepper
    - 1 tsp - Cumin
    - 1/2 tsp - Dried Sweet Basil Leaves
- 2 lbs - Boneless Skinless Chicken Breast
